Leg extension/Lying leg curl

Leg press

  • This machine helps targets the quads. Sit on seat with the back of the knees touching the front of the seat. Place legs under leg bar and slowly raise towards the sky then bring back down.
  • Targets the quads and glutes. Sit on seat with feet placed shoulder width apart on footstand. Push side lock handles outwards and bring knees towards chest, slowly push back up ensuring knees do not lock.

Back

Lat pull down

Cable pull down

  • Sit on bench with knees under knee pad; adjust if needed. Place hands on bar around the curved edge with thumbs facing one another. Slowly bring bar to your chest and make sure to keep back straight, chest out.
  • Place your feet shoulder width apart with a slight bend in the knees. Grab the rope or bar with a slight bend in the elbows. Bring down towards the middle of your quads/knees. Keep your head aligned with your spine.
